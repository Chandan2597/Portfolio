I'm Chandan, a dedicated Python and AI developer with a strong academic background in computer science and a passion for building intelligent, data-driven solutions. My technical skill set includes Python, GenAI, LLM, RAG, SQL, R, and C/C++, along with hands-on experience in machine learning, exploratory data analysis, hypothesis testing, NLP, and backend development. I am proficient in tools such as Tableau, Power BI, Advanced Excel, TensorFlow, Scikit-learn, MySQL, PostgreSQL, AWS, and Docker — enabling me to develop scalable applications and deliver meaningful insights from data.

With 1.5+ years of professional experience as a Python Developer at Nelle Vicinanze India Services Pvt. I also mentored students on data analysis, machine learning projects, and practical software development, helping bridge the gap between theoretical concepts and real-world applications.

I am analytical, adaptable, and committed to continuous learning. My goal is to leverage AI, backend development, and data-driven methodologies to build efficient, scalable solutions that drive meaningful impact.
